What are cookies?
A cookie is a small message sent from a website to your browser. The message is then stored in a file on your hard disk. A cookie file is small, usually 1kb. Cookies help the site recognize you.
How do they work?
Every time you "check in", two cookies are created in your computers memory (RAM) so that we can personalize the service to you.
