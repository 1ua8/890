1. Information We Collect

Our website does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The insurance calculator on our site operates entirely in your browser and does not transmit any data to our servers or third parties.

2. Calculator Data

All information entered into our insurance calculator (state, age, driving record, car type) is processed locally in your web browser. This data is not saved, stored, or transmitted to any external servers. Once you close your browser or refresh the page, all entered information is permanently deleted.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies to monitor user behavior or collect personal information. Our website is designed to be privacy-first and does not require any user accounts or registrations.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may display advertisements through Google AdSense. These third-party ad servers may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use our calculator or provide any information on our website.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
