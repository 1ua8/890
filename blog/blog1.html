2. Time Your Purchases Right

Most retailers follow predictable sales cycles. Electronics often go on sale in January (after CES) and July (back-to-school). Fashion items typically get discounted at the end of each season. Sign up for price alerts to be notified when items you want drop in price.

3. Use Cashback Websites

Combine coupon codes with cashback websites to double your savings. Some cashback portals offer up to 20% back on your purchases at certain retailers. This can add up to hundreds of dollars in savings annually.
